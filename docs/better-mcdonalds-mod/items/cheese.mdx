## Usage

### Food

Eating cheese restores 2 (<img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Hunger_%28icon%29.png" alt="Hunger icon" />) hunger and 0.4 [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).
