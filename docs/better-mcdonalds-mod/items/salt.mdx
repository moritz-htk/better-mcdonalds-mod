**Salt** is the [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from [salt blocks](../blocks/salt_block) used for crafting.

## Obtaining

### Mining

Salt is obtained when a [salt block](../blocks/salt_block) is broken with a [shovel](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Shovel). There are 2-4 salt in each block. If the shovel is enchanted with [Fortune](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fortune), it can drop an extra salt per level of Fortune. If the [salt block](../blocks/salt_block) is mined using a shovel enchanted with [Silk Touch](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Silk_Touch), it drops the [salt block](../blocks/salt_block) instead.
