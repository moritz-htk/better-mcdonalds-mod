**Raw bacon** is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) that can be eaten by the [player](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Player) or cooked in a [furnace](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Furnace), [smoker](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smoker), or [campfire](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Campfire) to make [cooked bacon](../ingredients/cooked_bacon).

## Obtaining

### Mob loot

When adult [pigs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pig) are killed, they drop 1-2 raw bacon. If killed while on fire, they drop [cooked bacon](../ingredients/cooked_bacon) instead.

## Usage

### Food

Eating raw bacon restores 3 (<img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Hunger_%28icon%29.png" alt="Hunger icon" /><img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Half_Hunger_%28icon%29.png" alt="Half Hunger icon" />) hunger and 0.4 [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).

### Wolves

Raw bacon can be used to [breed](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Breed) and heal tamed [wolves](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wolves), lead them around, and make baby tamed wolves grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
