**Cooked bacon** is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from [pigs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Pig), cooking [raw bacon](../ingredients/raw_bacon) or [trading](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Trading) with [villagers](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Villager).

## Obtaining

### Mob loot

Below is the chance for a [mob](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mob) to drop cooked bacon if the [entity](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Entity) is on fire when killed. Otherwise, [raw bacon](../ingredients/raw_bacon) is dropped instead.

### Cooking

[Raw bacon](../ingredients/raw_bacon) can be cooked in a [furnace](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Furnace), [smoker](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Smoker), or [campfire](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Campfire). Each cooked bacon removed from a furnace output slot gives 1 [experience](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Experience) (64 experience per stack).

## Usage

### Food

Eating cooked bacon restores 4 (<img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Hunger_%28icon%29.png" alt="Hunger icon" /><img src="https://minecraft.wiki/images/Hunger_%28icon%29.png" alt="Hunger icon" />) hunger and 0.4 [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).

### Wolves

Cooked bacon can be used to [breed](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Breed) and heal tamed [wolves](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wolves), lead them around, and make baby tamed wolves grow up faster by 10% of the remaining time.
