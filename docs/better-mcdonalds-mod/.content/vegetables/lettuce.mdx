# Lettuce

A **lettuce** is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from lettuce crops that can be used for farming and crafting.

## Obtaining

### Farming

When a fully-grown lettuce crop is harvested, it drops 1 lettuce and between 0 and 3 [lettuce seeds](@better_mcdonalds_mod:lettuce_seeds). A lettuce crop has a total of four growth stages (0-3) from planting until harvest. If a crop is harvested before it is fully grown, it drops only 1 seed.

## Usage

### Food

Eating a lettuce restores 1 (<img src="https://r2.sinytra.org/.assets/minecraft/icon/half_hunger.png" alt="Half Hunger icon" />) hunger and 0.3 [saturation](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hunger#Mechanics).

### Composting

Placing a lettuce into a [composter](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Composter) has a 65% chance of raising the compost level by 1.
